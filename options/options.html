Stock Ticker for Chrome will scan the current page that is being viewed for links to ticker symbols. When links are found a small bar will be displayed near the top of the page with each ticker symbol followed by relevant information such as the stock price or other metrics. The metrics shown are user configurable, so only the desired stock information is displayed.

Unlike other ticker bars Stock Ticker for Chrome is not real time. Metrics take several hours or longer to refresh. This is done intentionally. The purpose of the bar is to provide contextual information while browsing financial websites; information that will ideally be used to quickly dismiss a potential investment, resulting in more time spent researching stocks that present statistics already meeting a personal bare minimum required for further investigation. This is an investment tool designed to match only certain personalities and investment style. That being a slow, methodical, dispassionate research combined with a set of strict financial criteria.

Tips

  • Use Ctrl-Shift-O (Command-Shift-O on mac) to force open the tickerbar at any time.
      • If the above shortcut does not work:
    • Click on the 'customize and control' google chrome 3 bars in the upper right corner.
    • Select 'Settings'
    • Select 'Extensions'
    • Scroll to the bottom. In the lower right corner click on 'Keyboard Shortcuts'
    • Locate 'Show the ticker bar' and enter the shortcut key combination of your choosing.
