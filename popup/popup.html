Stock Ticker for Chrome is designed as an experiment to add value for stock symbols on a contextual basis. The extension is under heavy development, though as it is maintained by a single person updates may be slow. Please don't hesitate to leave feedback and offer suggestions.

Comments and suggestions are welcome, please leave your feedback at The Chrome Webstore.

Other users of this extension can be contact at the Google Group.

Just for fun you can try to see if anyone is online in the Google Hangout.

The latest development release can be found on github at https://github.com/delphian/chrome-stock-ticker
